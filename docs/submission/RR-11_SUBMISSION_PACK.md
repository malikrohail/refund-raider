# RR-11 Submission Pack

This folder locks the hackathon story, demo script, and promo copy for Refund Raider.

## Canonical Story

- Hero scenario: damaged, wrong, or missing ecommerce order with a public refund policy and an email support path.
- Demo promise: upload proof, research the policy, explain eligibility, draft the request, and send it.
- Proof rule: every verdict shown to the user must have visible evidence.

## Messaging Guardrails

- Keep the demo in the ecommerce refund wedge.
- Keep ElevenLabs as the advocate layer, not the workflow engine.
- Keep email as the primary send path.
- Keep the visual story anchored in evidence, verdict, draft, and send.
