# RR-11 Social Copy Starter

## Short Post

Built Refund Raider: an AI refund assistant that reads the policy, explains whether you qualify, drafts the refund request, and sends it for you.

The demo is simple on purpose:

- upload proof
- get evidence-backed verdict
- generate the email
- hit send

## Launch Post

Refund policies are buried, support is slow, and most people give up before asking for their money back.

Refund Raider fixes the boring part. It uses Firecrawl to find the policy and support path, ElevenLabs to explain the result, and email to send the actual refund request.

The best part: every verdict is backed by visible evidence.
