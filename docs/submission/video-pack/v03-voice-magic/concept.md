# v03-voice-magic

## Title

Talk to your refund advocate.

## Core hook

I built a voice agent that can look up the rules behind your refund and take the next step for you.

## Best use

Use only if the live ElevenLabs experience is genuinely strong.

## Audience

- judges
- X
- short demo clips

## Emotional arc

curiosity -> trust -> surprise

## Why it works

- makes ElevenLabs feel central
- turns the advocate layer into the star moment
- feels more magical than a normal app walkthrough

## Main risk

If the live voice exchange is slow or generic, the concept weakens fast.
