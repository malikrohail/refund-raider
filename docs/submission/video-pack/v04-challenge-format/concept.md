# v04-challenge-format

## Title

Can AI beat the refund maze in under a minute?

## Core hook

Can an AI go from broken package to sent refund request in under 60 seconds?

## Best use

Best short-form viral derivative.

## Audience

- TikTok
- Reels
- X

## Emotional arc

tension -> proof -> payoff

## Why it works

- gives the viewer a reason to stay
- feels very native to short-form platforms
- turns the demo into a challenge

## Main risk

The timer can overpower the product if the pacing gets too frantic.
