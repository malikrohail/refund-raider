# v04-challenge-format

## Core angle

`Can AI beat the refund maze in under a minute?`

## Best use

Best short-form viral derivative.

## Hook

`Can an AI go from broken package to sent refund request in under 60 seconds?`

## Why it works

- creates immediate tension
- highly native to short-form feeds
- gives the viewer a reason to keep watching

## 90-second beat sheet

### 0-5 seconds

Show a timer immediately.

### 5-15 seconds

Drop in:

- merchant
- problem
- proof

### 15-35 seconds

Show Firecrawl pulling policy evidence.

### 35-50 seconds

Show one short ElevenLabs explanation moment.

### 50-60 seconds

Show the draft.

### 60-70 seconds

Approve and send.

### 70-90 seconds

Fast recap:

`Policy found. Verdict made. Email sent.`

## Shot list

- `shot-01-hook`: timer and bold challenge text
- `shot-02-problem`: merchant card, complaint, proof
- `shot-03-firecrawl`: evidence stack and deadline
- `shot-04-elevenlabs`: one short spoken verdict caption
- `shot-05-draft`: preview of the request
- `shot-06-send`: send action
- `shot-07-end-card`: timer stop and recap slate
