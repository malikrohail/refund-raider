# v01-clean-winner

## Core angle

`Reads the rules. Gets your refund.`

## Best use

Main judged submission.

## Hook

`This AI reads the merchant's refund policy, tells you if you qualify, and sends the refund request for you.`

## Why it works

- instantly understandable
- strongest sponsor clarity
- easiest to judge
- cleanest 60-90 second structure

## 90-second beat sheet

### 0-5 seconds

Show the final case state first.

Narration:

`This AI reads the rules, figures out if you qualify, and sends the refund request for you.`

### 5-20 seconds

Show the damaged or wrong-item problem, proof, and merchant.

Narration:

`Most people lose money because refund policies are buried and support is annoying.`

### 20-40 seconds

Show Firecrawl evidence collection.

Narration:

`Refund Raider uses Firecrawl to find the real policy, support path, and deadline, then turns that into a usable verdict.`

### 40-60 seconds

Show the ElevenLabs advocate explaining the verdict.

Narration:

`ElevenLabs is the advocate layer. It explains what the rules mean and what the best next action is.`

### 60-80 seconds

Show the generated draft.

Narration:

`Then it drafts the refund request so the user does not have to write it.`

### 80-90 seconds

Approve and send. End on the timeline.

Narration:

`Evidence in. Refund out.`

## Shot list

- `shot-01-hook`: final case page with verdict, draft-ready state, send CTA
- `shot-02-problem`: damaged-item proof and one-line complaint
- `shot-03-firecrawl`: policy quote, deadline, support path
- `shot-04-elevenlabs`: short advocate explanation clip
- `shot-05-draft`: generated refund email
- `shot-06-send`: approve and send action
- `shot-07-end-card`: timeline with sent state
