# Audio Prompt Bank

Use this when generating narration and music after the concept is chosen.

## v01-clean-winner

### Voice direction

Calm, sharp, practical, low-hype advocate tone. Trustworthy and direct.

### Music direction

Subtle tech-operational bed. Light momentum, not cinematic drama.

## v02-frustration-to-relief

### Voice direction

Start tense and human, then settle into calm confidence after the verdict appears.

### Music direction

Begin with slight tension, resolve into cleaner, lighter momentum.

## v03-voice-magic

### Voice direction

Use the live ElevenLabs capture as the primary star. Bridge narration only where needed.

### Music direction

Very low or absent under the live voice exchange.

## v04-challenge-format

### Voice direction

Brisk, energetic, compressed, urgent without sounding chaotic.

### Music direction

Faster tempo with more punch and urgency.

## v05-policy-detective

### Voice direction

Analytical, credible, builder-friendly, more exact than flashy.

### Music direction

Restrained, minimal, credibility-first.
