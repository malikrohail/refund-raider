# v05-policy-detective

## Core angle

`Most AI tools stop at “here's the policy.” This one actually acts.`

## Best use

Credibility-forward version for judges, builders, and LinkedIn.

## Hook

`Most AI tools can summarize a policy. This one finds the evidence, explains if you qualify, drafts the request, and sends it.`

## Why it works

- makes the product feel more credible than generic AI demos
- emphasizes proof over hype
- strong builder and judge framing

## 90-second beat sheet

### 0-8 seconds

Open with skepticism:

`Most AI demos stop too early.`

### 8-25 seconds

Show the real refund problem and intake.

### 25-45 seconds

Show Firecrawl evidence and deadline extraction.

### 45-65 seconds

Show ElevenLabs translating the evidence into plain English.

### 65-85 seconds

Show draft and send.

### 85-90 seconds

End line:

`It does not just read the rules. It uses them.`

## Shot list

- `shot-01-hook`: bold skeptical opener over case page
- `shot-02-problem`: merchant and issue summary
- `shot-03-firecrawl`: evidence cards, quote, deadline
- `shot-04-elevenlabs`: plain-English qualification summary
- `shot-05-draft`: clear refund ask
- `shot-06-send`: sent state
- `shot-07-end-card`: proof-to-action thesis
