# v02-frustration-to-relief

## Title

I gave my refund fight to an AI.

## Core hook

Support kept making me do all the work, so I handed the whole refund fight to an AI agent.

## Best use

Emotional social cut or backup judged submission.

## Audience

- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- judges if kept disciplined

## Emotional arc

frustration -> relief -> empowerment

## Why it works

- starts from a human pain point
- feels more personal
- creates a strong before/after contrast

## Main risk

If it gets too personal, Firecrawl and ElevenLabs can become under-explained.
