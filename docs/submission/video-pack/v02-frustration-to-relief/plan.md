# v02-frustration-to-relief

## Core angle

`I gave my refund fight to an AI.`

## Best use

Emotional social cut or backup judged submission.

## Hook

`Support kept making me do all the work, so I handed the whole refund fight to an AI agent.`

## Why it works

- strong human pain point
- clear before/after transformation
- more native to TikTok and Reels

## 90-second beat sheet

### 0-8 seconds

Open on frustration:

- broken package
- ignored support thread
- visible annoyance

Narration:

`This is what getting a refund usually looks like.`

Then cut to:

`This is what it looks like when an agent does it for you.`

### 8-25 seconds

Open Refund Raider and drop in the case.

### 25-45 seconds

Show Firecrawl evidence and verdict formation.

### 45-65 seconds

Show ElevenLabs calmly explaining:

- you likely qualify
- here is the deadline
- here is the best path

### 65-85 seconds

Show draft, approve, and send.

### 85-90 seconds

End line:

`You should not need to become a policy investigator to get your money back.`

## Shot list

- `shot-01-hook`: messy support reality, broken item, unread thread
- `shot-02-shift`: hard cut from chaos to clean Refund Raider workspace
- `shot-03-firecrawl`: policy evidence, support path, deadline
- `shot-04-elevenlabs`: calm verbal explanation
- `shot-05-draft`: draft pane with clear ask
- `shot-06-send`: sent state
- `shot-07-end-card`: clean resolved frame

## Skills plan

### Product

- Firecrawl Search
- ElevenLabs live agent runtime

### Video

- `elevenlabs-remotion` for emotional narration pass
- `text-to-speech` for alternate opening-line takes
- `elevenlabs-music` for tension-to-relief scoring
- Remotion for before/after transitions
- `video-editing` for compression and emotional pacing
- `content-engine` for Reels/TikTok caption variants

## Risk

Low to medium

## Output target

- TikTok / Reels primary
- judge-safe backup if kept tight
