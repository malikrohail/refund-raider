# RR-11 Hero Scenario

## Locked Hero Scenario

`I bought something online. It arrived damaged, wrong, or missing. The merchant is slow, the policy is buried, and I want the fastest evidence-backed path to a refund.`

## Why This Is the Hero

- Immediate problem recognition.
- Easy to explain in one sentence.
- Strong visual demo: proof upload, policy lookup, verdict, draft, send.
- Fits the current product and sponsor stack.

## Demo Inputs

- Merchant name or URL.
- One-sentence issue summary.
- Proof upload such as receipt, photo, screenshot, or prior support email.
- Desired outcome, usually `full_refund`.

## Demo Output

- Clear verdict.
- Visible evidence cards.
- Drafted refund email.
- Send action.
- Status timeline update.

## Do Not Drift

- Do not shift the demo to subscriptions.
- Do not lead with chargebacks.
- Do not lead with phone support.
- Do not make the agent look decorative.
